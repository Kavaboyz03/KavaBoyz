Ingredients

Pure Powdered Kava

Traditional Method (How to Make)

  1. Add 1–2 spoons of kava powder into a strainer bag.
  2. Place the bag in a bowl with 2–3 cups warm water.
  3. Knead and squeeze for 5–10 minutes.
  4. Serve and enjoy.
